Our Legacy

Growing from a Passion for Traditions

Menaka Peiris Dance Academy was established in 2017 as a small traditional dance class under the guidance of the renowned actress and dancer, Mrs. Menaka Peiris.

Since our inception, we have grown into a trusted and respected dance academy with 10 branches islandwide, committed to excellence in Sri Lankan traditional dance education.
