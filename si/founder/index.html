H. Menaka Peiris

Menaka Peiris celebrated ශ්‍රී ලanka actress, professional dancer, model සහ performing artist. Television, cinema සහ dance වල contributions හරහා young performers inspire කරයි. Parapura, Aloko Udapadi, Minnu වැනි films වල appear වී versatile figure එකක්.

Young age සිට dance සහ performance love. Personal passion successful performing arts career එකක් බවට. Training, stage performances, media appearances හරහා valuable experience gain කර teaching philosophy foundation.

Performer සහ mentor ලෙස dance skill පමණක් නොව self-expression form එකක් — confidence, discipline, creativity, character build කරයි.

"සෑම නර්තන ශිlpīyakma unique කතාවක්. සෑම ශිෂ්‍යයෙකුටම voice discover කර confidence එක්ක express වී shine වීමට help කිරීම මගේ goal."

— මැනකා පීරිස්

  • Rupavahini State Award 2013 — Best Actress (Mala Teledrama)
  • Signis Salutation 2016 — Best Actress (Sath Paththini)
  • Signis Salutation 2023 — Best Actress (Paanamankada)
