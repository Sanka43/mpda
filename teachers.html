Founder & Lead Instructor

H. Menaka Peiris

Menaka Peiris is a celebrated Sri Lankan actress, professional dancer, model, and performing artist whose career has been built on a lifelong passion for the arts. Widely recognized for her contributions to television, cinema, and dance, she has inspired many young performers through her dedication, creativity, and commitment to excellence. She has appeared in productions such as the films Parapura, Aloko Udapadi, and Minnu, establishing herself as a versatile figure in Sri Lanka's entertainment industry.

From a young age, Mrs. Menaka demonstrated a deep love for dance and performance. What began as a personal passion eventually developed into a successful career in the performing arts. Through years of training, stage performances, media appearances, and artistic collaborations, she gained valuable experience that now forms the foundation of her teaching philosophy.
