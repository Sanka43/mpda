About Us

Established in 2017, we have grown into a trusted dance academy with 10 branches islandwide, offering comprehensive dance education for students aged 3 to 30.

Who Are We?

Menaka Peiris Dance Academy was established in 2017 as a small traditional dance class under the guidance of the renowned actress and dancer, Mrs. Menaka Peiris. Since our inception, we have grown into a trusted and respected dance academy that has captured the hearts of people across Sri Lanka. Today, we are proud to have expanded to 10 branches islandwide, supported by a team of professional instructors and dedicated teachers committed to excellence in Sri Lankan traditional dance education.

Our Mission

To preserve Sri Lankan cultural dance traditions while providing professional training that builds discipline, confidence, and artistic excellence in every student.

What We Offer

We provide comprehensive dance education for students aged 3 to 30, offering training in Udarata, Pahatharata, and Bollywood dance styles. Our programs are designed to develop strong technical skills, cultural understanding, and confident stage performance across all levels. In addition to practical training, we also provide theory knowledge of dance history and tradition. Our academy proudly hosts annual concerts and stage performances, giving students valuable real-world experience.

Because here, we are all a family.

Our Values

  • Tradition and cultural preservation
  • Discipline and dedication in learning
  • Artistic excellence and continuous improvement
  • Respect for teachers, students, and heritage
  • Passion for Sri Lankan traditional dance
  • Confidence building through performance
  • Creativity and self-expression
  • Friendship, kindness, and a supportive learning environment
  • Commitment to student growth and success

Our Vision

To be Sri Lanka's most respected traditional dance academy, nurturing confident performers who honor heritage while embracing innovation.
